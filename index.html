History

The Yadavs are a caste found primarily in India, Nepal, and Pakistan. They are traditionally a pastoral community and have a significant history that dates back to ancient times. The Yadavs have been noted in various historical texts as descendants of the deity Krishna and are associated with the Yadu dynasty.

Famous People

Lalu Prasad Yadav

An Indian politician from Bihar who has served as the Chief Minister of Bihar and the Minister of Railways.

Mulayam Singh Yadav

An Indian politician from Uttar Pradesh who founded the Samajwadi Party and served as the Chief Minister of Uttar Pradesh.

Rajpal Yadav

A well-known Bollywood actor known for his versatility and comic timing.
